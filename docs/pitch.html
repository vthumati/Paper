01 — The problem

Corporate legal is the founder's most expensive blind spot.

The work is unavoidable and highly regulated, yet the tooling is fragmented, manual and built for advisors — not for the people who actually run the company.

The cap table lives in a spreadsheet

Ownership, ESOP pools and convertibles drift out of sync the moment a round closes. Founders don't know their own fully-diluted position — and neither do their investors.

Compliance is a retainer and a prayer

ROC filings, board resolutions, FEMA (FC-GPR), statutory registers — outsourced to a CS, tracked nowhere, surfaced only when a deadline is already missed.

Fundraising means re-keying everything

Term sheets, PAS-4 offer letters, subscription agreements and the data room are rebuilt by hand each round, in Word, by a lawyer, at ₹lakhs a raise.

Funds run on Excel too

AIFs and syndicates manage LP capital accounts, drawdowns, the distribution waterfall, NAV and Form 64C/64D by hand — the same manual sprawl, one layer up.

02 — The product

Paper is the system of record for the whole company lifecycle.

Not a point tool bolted onto the workflow — the workflow itself. Every artifact reads from one source of truth, so the cap table, the compliance calendar and the fundraise never disagree — and when a fund backs a company that's also on Paper, that company's own numbers flow straight into the fund's monitoring.

07 — Why Paper wins

Global tools aren't built — or priced — for India. Local tools are point solutions.

Carta / Pulley / Ledgy
World-class cap tables, but US-priced and US-regulated — no PAS-4, no FC-GPR, no ROC, no SEBI AIF. A founder in Bengaluru pays in dollars for the wrong compliance.
Fund-ops tools (global)
Vestberry, 4Degrees, Carta fund admin — portfolio intelligence, deal CRM and fund accounting sold as three more dollar-priced subscriptions, none of them SEBI-native. Paper folds all three into the fund workspace.
Point tools (India)
Solve one slice — a cap table, or e-sign, or a compliance tracker. The founder still stitches five vendors together and re-keys data between them.
CS / CA retainers
The default today: reliable but manual, opaque and expensive, with the company's data trapped in someone else's inbox.
Paper
One India-native platform across the entire lifecycle — company and fund — with compliance built in and a services marketplace when a human is genuinely needed.
